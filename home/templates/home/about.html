About

How it began...

Neat was founded in 2014 by two friends, Jane Smith and Wanda Taylor after meeting at university where they studied psychology. They wanted to create a brand that would not only empower people to be their best, organized selves but would become a staple in someone's everyday life.

Products with purpose...

Materials and design matter. At Neat we are obsessed with finding the best materials and crafting them into both elegant and functional organizational tools for your everyday life. What you get in the mail will feel elevated and thoughtful and we make sure that every product gets the love and attention it deserves. We want every item to have a meaningful purpose in your life. By choosing our products you are not only getting organized but you are joining a community of people who are sharing all the ways they are staying organized and what helps them be and feel successful.
